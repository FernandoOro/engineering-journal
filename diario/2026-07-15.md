## Learnings / technical decisions
- When an infrastructure blocker doesn't yield to more resources (two machines), the real way out
  can be **reducing the problem** (running less) instead of scaling it up (running on more
  hardware).
- Documenting a response-field addition in the spec the same day it's added — while context is
  fresh — avoids having to reconstruct it later.

## What I could do better
- Spent both Tuesday **and** Wednesday on the two-machine networking idea before trying the
  simpler option (partial containers). Should have tried "run less" first — it's the cheaper thing
  to attempt — before setting up a network between two machines.
